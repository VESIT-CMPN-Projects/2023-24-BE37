Our Work

Who we are

We are a group of passionate students dedicated to fetal brain anomaly detection. Driven by our commitment to advancing healthcare and leveraging our skills and knowledge, we aim to contribute meaningfully to the field of fetal health.

Why choose us?

Our approach to fetal brain anomaly detection is grounded in robust foundations. We provide comprehensive datasets that expedite research, backed by our deep expertise in fetal brain anatomy, ensuring precision. Diverse data sources enhance our adaptability, while gestational age independence guarantees reliable results. Our system is also adaptive to various fetal positions, maintaining consistent accuracy. In essence, we're committed to early and precise anomaly detection, making us the dependable choice.
